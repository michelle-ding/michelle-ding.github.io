# Michelle L. Ding
Hi! I'm Michelle, a researcher and community organizer critically investigating how AI systems impact communities. I am currently a Computer Science Ph.D student at Brown University advised by [Suresh Venkatasubramanian](https://vivo.brown.edu/display/suresh) and [Harini Suresh](https://harinisuresh.com/). I am affiliated with the [Center for Tech Responsibility](https://cntr.brown.edu/) and the [Data in Society Collective Lab](https://discolab.cs.brown.edu/). My work broadly discusses topics in socio-technical AI governance, intersections of gender and technology, tech accountability, and cross-stakeholder/cross-disciplinary translation and bridge building.
